# POLYCC Agentic AI League 2026

## Official Competition Information

### Organizer

POLYCC (Jabatan Pendidikan Politeknik dan Kolej Komuniti)

Supported by AWS.

---

## Competition Objective

Participants are expected to learn and build AI Agents using AWS tools provided through AWS Workshop.

The competition focuses on:

- Agent Design
- Problem Solving
- Decision Making
- Agentic Thinking
- AI Workflow Design

---

## Eligibility

Officially confirmed:

- Open to all POLYCC students currently studying.
- Open to students who have recently completed industrial training.
- No technical background required.
- No programming background required.
- No special hardware required.
- No software installation required.

---

## Team Composition

Each team consists of:

- 1 Lecturer (Mentor)
- 2 Students

A lecturer may mentor multiple teams.

---

## AWS Resources

Officially confirmed:

Each team will receive free access to:

### AWS Workshop

AWS web-based environment provided for competition use.

Possible tools include:

- SageMaker AI Studio
- Bedrock AgentCore

---

## Competition Structure

### Stage 1

Institution Screening

All teams from the same institution may participate.

No limit on the number of teams.

---

### Stage 2

Institution Selection

Only ONE team with the highest score from each institution will become the institution representative.

---

### Stage 3

National Selection

Top scoring teams across institutions are evaluated.

Five teams with the highest scores and attendance confirmation will advance to the final.

Note:

The final number may change depending on venue capacity.

---
